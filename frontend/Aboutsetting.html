About us

Welcome to the official Room Booking System of the Royal University of Phnom Penh (RUPP) — a modern, digital solution developed to simplify and streamline the process of reserving university spaces.

Our Purpose

At RUPP, we understand the importance of having accessible and well-managed spaces for learning, collaboration, and academic events. This platform was created to help students, faculty, and staff easily book rooms for:

  • 1. Group study or research
  • 2. Departmental meetings
  • 3. Workshops or training sessions
  • 4. University events or presentations

What This System Offers

Our web-based booking system is designed to be:

  • Simple & user-friendly – Book a room in just a few clicks
  • Transparent – View available rooms and time slots in real time
  • Efficient – Save time with instant confirmations and automated scheduling
  • Fair – Prevent double-bookings and promote equal access for all

Who Can Use It?

The system is available for:

  • 1. RUPP Students – For academic group work, project collaboration, and exam prep
  • 2. Lecturers & Faculty – For classes, departmental activities, or office hours
  • 3. University Staff – For meetings and official purposes

Responsible Use

We encourage all users to respect the booking system by:

  • 1. Only booking rooms when needed
  • 2. Arriving on time and leaving the room clean
  • 3. Cancelling bookings if no longer needed
  • 4. Misuse of the system may result in temporary booking restrictions.
